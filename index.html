800 Words

March

I will start writing 800+ words per day. This should help me in several ways: reduction of stress, working through complex problems and issues, and possibly leaving something for the family to reflect on and/or remember me by.

Mobile App

April

I will fully use my skills learned from One Month on Ruby on Rails and HTML to start development of my first mobile app for the iPhone. I will have a version 1.0 ready to go live by the end of the month.

Networking

May

At this event, I will do my best to come out of my shell more and do some much-needed networking - not only for my existing real estate investment business, but for my future career plans of becoming a full-fledged web and mobile app developer.

Learn Python

Spa Treatment

July
